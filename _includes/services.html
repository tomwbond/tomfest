Run all day, party all night

TomFest is a festival that blends movement, music, and fun. Whether you’re a seasoned runner or just getting started, there’s something here for you!

Move

Join guided runs for every level and running-focused strength and yoga workshops. Enjoy the chaos of the Good Boy Run for Dogs and the Rave Run and complete the epic Beer Mile relay.

Learn

Discover new ways to prevent and treat injury and elevate your running performance. Hear from experts on mental health and running form and from inspiring runners about how they do what they do.

Party

Dive into delicious local food and drink then dance the night away to eclectic live music. Celebrate with friends new and old.
